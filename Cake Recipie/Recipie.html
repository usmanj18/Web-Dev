Chocolate Cake Recipie

Ingridients:

  • Flour 5g
  • Sugar 8g
  • Flavour 10g
  • Chocolate 15g
  • Bread 5pc

Instructions:

  1. Mix the flour with Bread
  2. wait for 5 min
  3. Add Flavour and Sugar
  4. Pour chocolate over it
  5. Bake it for 5min and enjoy :)
